Torrent Suite™ Assay Development Software Help

Pre-installed panels

The following pre-installed panels are available in Torrent Suite™ Assay Development Software.

Research panel/kit

Description

Oncomine™ Focus Assay

A multi-biomarker assay that enables the detection of variants in 52 genes relevant to solid tumors. This assay allows concurrent analysis of DNA and RNA to simultaneously detect multiple types of variants, single nucleotide variants (SNVs), indels, copy number variants (CNVs) and gene fusions, in a single workflow.

Solid Tumour DNA Kit

A single pool of primers designed to amplify 92 amplicons covering more than 1800 COSMIC mutations from 22 genes associated with solid tumors.

Solid Tumour Fusion Transcript Kit

A single pool of primers designed to detect rearrangements involving ALK, ROS-1, RET, and NTRK-1 genes associated with solid tumors.

Ion AmpliSeq™ Cancer Hotspot Panel v2

A single pool of primers used to perform multiplex PCR for preparation of amplicon libraries from genomic "hot spot" regions that are frequently mutated in human cancer genes.

Ion AmpliSeq™ BRCA1 and BRCA 2 Panel

Utilizes 167 amplicons to analyze the coding regions of both BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes.

Ion AmpliSeq™ CFTR Panel

A two-pool panel with 102 amplicons used for re-amplification and analysis of the coding region of the CFTR gene. This panel allows you to analyze all coding exons, intron-exon boundaries and UTRs of CFTR, including the >160 mutations from CFTR2.org research database.

Ion AmpliSeq™ Colon and Lung Research Panel v2

A single-pool panel that analyzes hotspot and targeted regions of 22 genes implicated in colon and lung cancers. This panel uses 92 amplicons for re-sequencing and analysis.

Ion AmpliSeq™ RNA Fusion Lung Cancer Research Panel

Designed to target a set of known fusion transcripts as well as expression imbalances between the 3' and 5' regions of the genes. The panel contains 85 amplicons in a single pool.

Ion AmpliSeq™ AML Panel

A four-pool panel that utilizes 237 amplicons to analyze 19 genes implicated in AML (entire coding regions: CEPBA, DNMT3A, GATA2, TET2, TP53; hotspot regions: ASXL1, BRAF, CBL, FLT3, IDH1, IDH2, JAK2, KIT, KRAS, NPM1, NRAS, PTPN11, RUNX1, WT1).

16S Metagenomics Kit

Enables the identification (at genus or species level) of microbes present in complex polybacterial samples, using both the curated Greengenes and premium curated MicroSEQ® ID 16S rRNA reference databases.
