View the Results Report

The Results Report screen displays all the data and results from a sequencing run or reanalysis of a sample.

To view the Results Report :

  1. In the Data tab > Completed Runs & Results Screen , locate the sample, then click on the number in the Results column to the view the Results List screen.

    In the Results List screen, results are listed by Planned Run. Results generated by reanalysis are indicated in the Assay column with the reanalysis assay name and the symbol . Other features of the screen are described in View the Results List.

  2. In the Action column, click View Result for the desired run or reanalysis.

    The Result Report screen is divided into tabs.

    Note:

    The QC Status (Passed or Failed) is listed above the tabs. Failure to meet or exceed any QC metric defined in the assay will yield a failed result.

  3. Click the applicable tab to view the tables and figures of results.
