Monitoring runs

In the Monitor tab, users can view the status of any jobs running on instruments connected to the Ion PGM™ Torrent Server. To monitor the status of runs versus instruments, click Run View or Instrument View under the tab.

Run information is displayed:

  • For 72 hours after starting the Ion OneTouch™ Dx Instrument or Ion Chef™ Instrument run (including QC results following completion of data analysis), after which all results are only accessible under the Data tab > Completed Runs & Results screen.

  • For 14 days after starting the Ion OneTouch™ Dx Instrument or Ion Chef™ Instrument if data analysis is not completed, after which it is removed from the Monitor tab.
