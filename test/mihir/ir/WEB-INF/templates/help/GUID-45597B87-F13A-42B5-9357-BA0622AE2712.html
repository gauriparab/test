Allele coverage does not match Hotspot coverage

The coverage value reported under the Variants tab and Allele Coverage tab can be different.

No action required. The coverage value reported under the Variants tab is the coverage after down-sampling, while the Allele Coverage tab reports the raw coverage without down-sampling. Down-sampling can speed up variant calling for some over-sampled positions. The down-sampling threshold for Ion PGM™ Dx variant calling is 400.
