Manage copy number baselines

In the Assay tab > Presets screen > Copy Number Baselines subtab, available baselines are listed in the table.

  • To view the details of a CNV baseline, click on the baseline name.

  • To import a CNV baseline file, click Import and select the file.

  • To make a baseline unavailable in the software, click Obsolete in the Actions column.

  • To view the log file for the CNV baseline, click View Log.

  • To export a baseline, click Export.
