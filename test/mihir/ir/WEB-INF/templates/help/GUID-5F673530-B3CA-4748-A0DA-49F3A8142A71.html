View active jobs

To view or terminate an Active Job:

  1. Click Terminate to stop the active job.

    A confirmation dialog will open "Are you sure you want to terminate Job Name ?"

  2. Click the red Terminate button to terminate the job.

    Click Cancel to return to the Services page without terminating the job.
