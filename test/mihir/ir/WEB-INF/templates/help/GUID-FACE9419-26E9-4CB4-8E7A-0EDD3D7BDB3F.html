Manage hotspot files

In the tab > References screen > Hotspots subtab, you can view the hotspot BED files available in the software.

  • To add a new hotspot file, click Add New and enter a name, select a genome reference, and select the BED file containing the hotspots information. Click Save when done.
    Note:

    Extremely large files take time to upload and may not appear immediately.

  • To export a hotspot file, click Export in the Actions column.
  • To obsolete a hotspot file, making it unavailable in the software, click Obsolete in the Actions column. Pre-installed hotspot files cannot be obsoleted.
