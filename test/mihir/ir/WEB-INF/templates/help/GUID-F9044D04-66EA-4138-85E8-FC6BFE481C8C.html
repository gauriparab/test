Edit sample details

In the Samples tab > Manage Samples screen, editable samples are identified by the Edit button under the Sample ID. Samples that have been assigned to a library are locked and cannot be edited or deleted.

Note:

The Sample ID cannot be edited.

To edit a sample:

  1. Click Edit in the row for the sample to be edited.

    The Edit Sample dialog will open.

  2. Click in the desired field to edit the information, then click Save.
