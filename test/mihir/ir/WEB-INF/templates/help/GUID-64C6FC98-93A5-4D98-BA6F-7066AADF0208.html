Download and print reports

Reports can be downloaded to save, view, and print from the Results report window:

  1. Click Lab Report. The report will automatically download in .pdf format.

    Electronically signed-off reports have an "Approved" watermark and the Sign Off details are included in the footer.

  2. Click View Results.
    1. Click Download Files.
    2. Select Lab_Report.pdf, then click Download.

      A .zip folder containing all the selected files will download to your desktop.

  3. Open the downloaded PDF file in an appropriate viewer, then print from within the open document.
