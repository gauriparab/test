Sign off verification run reports (Manager/Administrator only)

Managers and Administrators can sign off on results reports for Verification runs. However, we recommend that only qualified support specialists sign off on PQ reports.

  1. In the Report window, click on the Sign Off button in the upper right corner above the report.

    The Sign off Electronic Signature dialog will open.

  2. Enter your User Name and Password and any comments, then click Sign Off.
  3. The report will be locked and listed in the PQ Report field for the run in the Verification Runs window.
  4. Click on the PQ Report name to view, print or download the report as a PDF.
