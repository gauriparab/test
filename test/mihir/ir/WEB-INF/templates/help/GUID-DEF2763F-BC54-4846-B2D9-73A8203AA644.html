Delete an Assay

When a Manager or Administrator first creates an assay, it will be listed as "Draft" in the Status column in the Manage Assays screen. While the assay is in draft status, you can delete it.

Note:

Only draft assays can be deleted. Once an assay has been locked, it can be obsoleted to remove it from future use.

Click on Delete below the assay name in the Manage Assays list, then confirm the deletion.
