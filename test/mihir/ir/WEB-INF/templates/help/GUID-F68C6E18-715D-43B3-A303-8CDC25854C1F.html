Enter the extraction kit barcode

In the Samples tab > Manage Samples screen, you can enter the barcode of the kit used to extract each sample. This barcode is saved with the sample and can be viewed by clicking on the Sample ID.

  1. Above the samples list, click To Be Extracted to display only those samples that do not have an extraction kit barcode associated with them.
  2. Select the checkbox of the sample you have extracted using a particular kit. You can also select multiple samples if they used the same kit.
  3. Click Extract. In the dialog box, enter the barcode printed on the box of the extraction kit.
  4. Click Save. The sample will no longer be listed in the To Be Extracted list.
