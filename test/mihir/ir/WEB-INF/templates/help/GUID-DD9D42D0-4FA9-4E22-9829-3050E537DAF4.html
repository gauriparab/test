Search samples

To search for samples, in the Samples tab > Manage Samples screen:

  1. Enter the full, or partial, sample ID into the Sample ID field.
  2. Click Search.

    The sample(s) matching the search parameters will be listed.

  3. Click Clear to return to the complete list of samples.
