Sign off on a Results Report

To open the Results Report for the sample of interest:

  1. Click the number under the Results column header to open the list of Result Reports.
    Note:

    Result reports generated by reanalysis are indicated in the Assay column with the Reanalysis assay name and the symbol .

  2. Under the Actions column header click View Result.
  3. Click on each tab in the report to view the information.
  4. To sign off the result report:
    1. Click Sign Off, then enter user name, password, and comments in the dialog window. Fields identified with a red asterisk (*) are required.
    2. Click Sign Off to confirm your electronic signature.

      The Sign Off Details will appear in the QC Report tab and downloaded PDF report.
