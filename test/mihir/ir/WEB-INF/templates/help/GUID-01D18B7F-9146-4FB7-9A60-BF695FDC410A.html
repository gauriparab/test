Review the assay audit trail

Managers and administrators can review and export the details of all the actions performed on an assay.

  1. In the Manage Assays screen, click Audit under the assay name.
  2. Each action performed on the assay is listed in the Audit Trail dialog. Click in the Record column to view the details of each action.
  3. In the Audit Record Details dialog, click Export to export a PDF of the selected record.
Note:

To view and export the audit trail of a Reanalysis assay, in the Manage Assays screen, click the number under the Reanalysis column of the assay to open the Reanalysis assay list.
