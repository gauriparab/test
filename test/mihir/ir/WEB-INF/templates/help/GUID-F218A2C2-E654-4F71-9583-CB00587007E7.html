Update the Audit Configuration

Audit Configuration allows the Administrator to require that a reason for the change is included as part of making a change to designated objects. To update the Audit Configuration:

  1. Click on the Audit Configuration button.

    The Audit Configuration dialog will open.

  2. Click in the Require Reason checkbox adjacent to the Data Object Name.
  3. Click Save.

    To remove a reason for change requirement, open the Audit Configuration dialog, deselect the checkbox and click Save.
