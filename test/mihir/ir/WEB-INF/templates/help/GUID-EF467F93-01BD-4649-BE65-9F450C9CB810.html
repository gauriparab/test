Review Sample History

The entire history of a sample is available for review, export, or printing.

  1. In the Samples tab > Manage Samples screen, under the Sample ID of the sample of interest, click Audit.

    The Audit Trail dialog window will open. Listed in the window are each modifying event for the selected sample.

  2. Click on the details icon under the Record header to view the details of the change made.

    The Audit Record Details dialog will open detailing the edits made.

  3. In the Audit Record Details dialog, click:
    1. Export to export a print ready PDF of the record.
    2. Cancel to return to the Audit Trail window.
  4. Click Cancel to return to the Manage Samples screen.
