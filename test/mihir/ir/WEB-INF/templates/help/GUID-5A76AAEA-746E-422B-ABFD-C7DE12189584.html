Stop ionCrawler service

The ionCrawler service can be disabled if required.

  1. Below the ionCrawler Service Details header, click Stop.

    A confirmation dialog window will open, " Are you sure you want to Stop ionCrawler service ?"

  2. Click Yes to confirm stopping the service function.

    The ionCrawler Service Details pane will collapse and the option Start will replace Stop.

  3. Click No to return to the Services page.
  4. To reactivate the ionCrawler service click Start.

    A confirmation dialog window will open, " Are you sure you want to Start ionCrawler service?"

  5. Click Yes to confirm restarting the ionCrawler service function.
