Configure Email Settings

Within the Ion PGM™ Torrent Server Email Settings tab configure the server to allow sending email notifications. To enter the required information:

  1. Obtain SMTP Server URL and Port information from your IT department.
  2. Click in a designated field, then enter the appropriate value.

    Required fields are indicted with a red asterisk (*).

  3. Click Update.
  4. Click Reset to return the Email Settings to the default values.
  5. Click Send Test Email to send a test email to the email address provided in your user account.
