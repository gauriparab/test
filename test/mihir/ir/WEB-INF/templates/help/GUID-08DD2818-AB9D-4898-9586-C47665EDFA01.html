Obsolete an Assay (Manager/Administrator)

Note:

You can only obsolete locked assays.

Making an assay obsolete removes the assay from use in the software. The assay is not deleted and a record of it is maintained in the audit trail. The results for any runs already performed with the assay will remain in the database.

  1. Click Obsolete under the Actions header.
  2. Click Yes to confirm the deletion.
