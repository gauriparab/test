Lock an assay

When a Manager or Administrator first creates an assay, it will be listed as "Draft" in the Status column in the Manage Assays screen. To use the assay, you need to lock it.

In the Actions column for the Assay, click on Lock. Locked assays cannot be edited or deleted.
