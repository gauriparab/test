Search and sort assays

In the Manage Assays screen, the most recently created assay is listed first by default. To return to the default display, click Manage Assays to refresh the screen.

  • To search the assay list:
    1. Enter the full, or partial, assay name into the Assay Name field.
    2. Click Search.

      The assay(s) matching the search parameters will be listed.

    3. Click Clear to return to the complete list of assays.
  • To sort the assay list:
    1. Click on the desired column header.

      The list of assays will reorder based on the column.

    2. Click on the header name a second time to reverse the order.
